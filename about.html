About PENTAGON

PENTAGON is a South Korean music group consisting of nine members. They made their debut on October 6, 2016 under CUBE Entertainment.

Jinho

Jinho is the oldest member and main vocalist of PENTAGON. He is most known for his soulful and professional singing talent. He has been thriving in the industry for more than 10 years and also works as a vocal trainer for younger singers.

Hui

Hui is the leader, main vocalist and main producer of PENTAGON. Most of the group's songs are composed by Hui. Hui not only composes songs for his group but also for other artists.

Hongseok

Hongseok is the third main vocalist in the group. He is most well-known for his warm healing voice. He is also an actor.

Shinwon

Shinwon is a composer, rapper, vocalist with husky voice. He is a passionate music genius who can play a lot of instruments. He is also a professional runway model.

Yeo One

Yeo One is the lead vocalist of PENTAGON. He is known for his low-tone stable vocals. He is also a professional guitar player who used to be in a high school rock band.

Yanan

Yanan is high-tone vocalist who carries the high notes in their songs along with the main vocalists. He is Chinese and also popular among fans for his breathtaking visuals.

Yuto

Yuto is a main rapper and a Japanese member. He has a very deep voice that adds a unique colour to their songs. Unlike his gigantic dark appearence, he writes the most beautiful and poetic songs for the group.

Kino

Kino is the main dancer of the group with outstanding dancing skills. Most of dance moves in their performances are choreographed by him. He is also an excellent composer who is behind the most loved PENTAGON songs.

Wooseok

Wooseok is a main rapper and composer. He contributes most of the group's discography along with the leader Hui. He is the youngest and tallest member of the group.
